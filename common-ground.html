“Common Ground” is my ongoing exploration of the connection between the European landscape and its political implications. A fundamental goal of the European idea is to share this continent in peace. But in times of environmental crises and strong international tensions, this objective is under attack from many sides. With this work I search for reflections of these struggles in the landscape that surrounds me.

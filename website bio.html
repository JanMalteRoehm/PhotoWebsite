About

Jan Malte Röhm is a German photographer based in Rotterdam, the Netherlands. His work evolves mainly around social topics and can be placed in documentary photography but in contrast to the tradition of this genre his pictures are not supposed to strictly resemble what we see. Rather he aims to reach people on an emotional level to connect with the topics he wants to talk about.
